Basic Information

Learn more from our website so you can help us to encourage people around you to adopt a pet rather than buying animals.
What is PetMeOut?

PetMeOut is a pet adoption platform connecting Adoptee and Adopters. By using this website, you will be able to find adoptable cute animals that desperately need a new home.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to simplify the whole pet adoption process to the next level by using technology.

Who should use this website?

Everyone who loves animals, everyone who want to adopt animals, everyone who wants to find adopters and everyone who wants to participate to solve animals overpopulation and give more chances for the stray or rescued animals to have a better life.

Are we allowed to buy and sell?

NO! we are totally against animals trade and we are fully supporting the stray animal adoption program.

Is there another languages?

Our website is using English as a global main language. We are looking to provide more languages in the future.
