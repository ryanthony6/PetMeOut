Basic Information

Learn more from our website so you can help us to encourage people around you to adopt a pet rather than buying animals.
What is PetMeOut?

PetMeOut is a pet adoption platform connecting Adoptee and Adopters. By using this website, you will be able to find adoptable cute animals that desperately need a new home.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to simplify the whole pet adoption process to the next level by using technology.

Who should use this website?

Everyone who loves animals, everyone who want to adopt animals, everyone who wants to find adopters and everyone who wants to participate to solve animals overpopulation and give more chances for the stray or rescued animals to have a better life.

Are we allowed to buy and sell?

NO! we are totally against animals trade and we are fully supporting the stray animal adoption program.

Is there another languages?

Our website is using English as a global main language. We are looking to provide more languages in the future.

Adoption

By adopting a rescue or stray animals, you will get a forever-loyal companion.
How to adopt a cat/dog in PetMeOut?

When you register to PetMeOut. You will have both controls as an Adopter and as an Adoptee. Adopters are users who use PetMeOut as pet adoption app to find a companion animal. All you have to do is to fill the adoption form on the animal's profile and get connected with the Adoptee once your application to adopt has been accepted.

How to find adopters for my rescued animals?

When you register to PetMeOut. You will have both controls as an Adopter and as an Adoptee. Adoptees are users who use PetMeOut as pet adoption app to find adopters for the animals. Each adoptee represents 1 or more animals. All you have to do is start uploading the animal's photo and information and share. Once your animals have been uploaded, you may receive some adoption applications answers from potential adopters. Make sure you accept the most suitable adopters for your animals by screening the best information you get.
